This website records all the noise complaints received by the authorities in NYC in 2023 that were reported by calling 311. It exists to give an overview of the kind of noise that a person may witness while travelling through specific areas in NYC and it also shows a comparison of three individuals' personal experiences relating to noise in their respective neighborhoods, i.e. The Upper East Side, Bushwick and Long Island City.
